About our Apartment

​❖​ On Sorrento at the Mornington Peninsula (one hour from Melbourne) next door to Portsea.

❖ Our apartment is a lazy 10-minute walk from the relaxed township of Sorrento that offers restaurants, cafes, art & crafts, farmers' market and lots more. 

❖ Only 4 houses from the beach in a quiet avenue, easy walking distance to the ocean beaches and surf. 

❖ We offer a spacious living area overlooking a beautiful garden.

❖ Queen sized bedroom ​with top quality amenities include bedding, fluffy towels, bath robes and vanity packs.

❖ ​Modern, elegant bathroom has a luxurious bath that entices you to unwind and relax whilst overlooking a private courtyard fernery. 

❖ ​A separate kitchenette has everything you need to make your stay relaxed and comfortable.   We include a coffee machine and a variety of coffee pods for your enjoyment. 

❖ Private entrance allows you to come and go as you please.

❖ Two TVs in all units to make sure everyone happy!

❖ Undercover BBQ area with seating offers plenty of space to stretch out and enjoy that cup of freshly brewed coffee or a glass of wine.

❖ Car parking is close by with room for a boat as well!
